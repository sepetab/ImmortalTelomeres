Healthhacker Workshop - Health messaging and coding of disease classification

The workshop invloves talks on coding and classification systems for medical data (SNOMED, ICD-10-AM & DICOM) and on messaging standards (FHIR, HL7, Continua Alliance).

Please read our schedule to learn more.

Venue

The workshop is held at UNSW's built environment. Workshop programme is available here. Restaurants and recreation facilities are available. Due to pandemic, masks and vaccination report mandatory.

Abstract submissions
